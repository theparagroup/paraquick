--[sequences]
	Main Sequence:

		serverVersion
		clientVersion
		authenticate
			sendRequestXML -> <xml>
					receiveResponseXML -> (-1)
						getLastError
						closeConnection
				OR
					receiveResponseXML -> (less than 100)
						sendRequestXML
							...
				OR
					receiveResponseXML -> (100)
						closeConnection

	----------------------------

	QB Connection Error Sequence:

		serverVersion
		clientVersion
		authenticate
				connectionError -> "done"
					closeConnection?    
			OR
				connectionError -> "<path to company file>"
					connectionError
						... (called till max retries exceeded)

	----------------------------

	Interactive Mode Sequence:

		serverVersion
		clientVersion
		authenticate
			sendRequestXML -> ""
				getLastError
					getInteractiveURL
							interactiveRejected (user cancels)
						OR
							interactiveDone (polled)
					closeConnection?

	----------------------------
